About NeoScreen



Who We Are

     Neoscreen was created to fix a problem that needed to be dealt with now. Millions of people in the world have to deal with broken phone screens every single day. This threatens the safety many adults and children and the overall condition of a broken phone. This problem can only be solved with by replacing the screen, which is expensive, time consuming, and can even damage the interior of a phone.

     We asked ourselves, how can we prevent these problems that arise when a phone screen is broken, keep it affordable, and make the process easy? That is when we came up with NeoScreen, the easy and affordable way prevent your broken phone screen from further damage and to protect your fingers getting cut when using it.

     NeoScreen is a thin film made out of a low-melting point plastic. All you need to do is place it on top of the screen, blow dry it for 60 seconds, and you are done. When it cools and hardens, your screen now has a protective layer between the shattered glass and your fingers.
